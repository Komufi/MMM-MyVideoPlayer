You must download youtube videos and the ending MUST be .mp4
Must to have the names of the videos in either single words or separated by an underscore
IE: One.mp4, Video_One.mp4, My_First_Video.mp4
Put them in your videos directory

put any images in the posters folder, using the same  'name' (video= one.mp4, image = one.png or one.jpg)
